SimCity (2013) is the city building and simulation computer game developed by Maxis and published by Electronic Arts. It is the latest title in the SimCity franchise. SimCity was officially released on March 5th 2013 in United States and March 8th in Europe for Microsoft Windows. Mac version was released on August 29, 2013.
